Meet Dani Michelle, the Stylist Behind Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber's Looks

Style lesson!

BY JESSICA SCEMAMA October 4, 2024

Exclusive meeting with Dani Michelle, stylist to the stars and now jewelry designer

Vogue. What’s your earliest jewelry memory?

Dani Michelle. “I think my earliest memory is when I was 13, when my grandfather, who I didn’t know very well at the time, gave me a heart-shaped necklace for my birthday. I remember staring at it, thinking it was the most meaningful gift he could give me. I knew it was something I would have forever, that I could wear as a token of his love."

Dani Michelle

What is your most treasured piece of jewelry? The piece that is closest to my heart is a solid gold flat link bracelet that my grandmother used to wear. It is one of the main inspirations for our current collection, and it always reminds me of her.

What is the one you never leave home without?

I never leave home without all my rings.

How did your brand MÉGÀ come about?

It was actually Vogue who called me a “Megawatt” stylist, and at that moment I knew it was time to spread my wings further. I called my mother and asked her if she would create a line with me since she is an expert in jewelry.

How has your own style influenced your jewelry?

My own style is timeless, cool, and sophisticated. I hope that’s what MEGÀ also exudes. I like to mix metals and stack them. In this sense, we designed the jewelry to be worn together and layered. In fact, each piece is part of a collective vision.

Who would you like to see wearing your jewelry?

I dream of Kate Moss wearing our MEGA pieces. We actually have a model named after her.

What piece of jewelry do you think will enhance even the simplest outfit?

A good pair of earrings will complete any outfit. They can even take your outfit from day to night. The bigger they are, the more festive they are. Choose them with a few extra diamonds and you're done!

What jewelry piece do you think is the most daring? And how do you make it your own?

Maxi earrings here too! But all the jewelry we wear is. No matter how you wear it, it becomes yours. And because it reveals a little bit of your personality, it is daring.

What advice would you give to someone who is averse to wearing jewelry or only wears classic pieces?

I would start with a snake chain like our Omega necklace. It is timeless, chic, and minimalist. It manages to add allure to a look without overdoing it thanks to the shimmer of gold, while being very easy to wear.

What are the 3 pieces that a woman should always have in her jewelry box?

Large earrings, again, smaller models, and a ring to wear on the little finger.

For a first jewelry investment, what piece would you recommend?

Again, I would say a snake chain necklace. It can be worn every day or to enhance more formal looks. I wear three of them and I never take them off.

What are MÉGÀ’s next projects?

An upcoming collaboration with a girl who inspires me a lot. To be continued.
